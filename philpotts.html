PHILPOTTS

Website Re-design

Based out of Honolulu, Hawaii, Philpotts Interiors is a nationally awarded interior design agency, that specializes in creating spaces that reflect the spirit of their surroundings. Founded in the 1960’s by Mary Philpotts McGrath, Philpotts Interiors began by catering to the clients of newly constructed buildings and residences that were springing up all over the islands. With over a half century in the business, and with the same goal of creating location that have a “sense of place” since its founding,the Philpotts team continues to seek work that allows them to design spaces that not only leave lasting impression on their visitors, but that also breathes life and energy into them.

The purpose of the re-design for Philpotts Interiors website was to create a unique visual experience that followed the company motto of a “sense of place”. By utilizing white space and a layout that removed unnecessary clutter, the new site allows users to travel between pages while experiencing different visual structures. Keeping mind that each user of the site would be visiting with different agendas, a new navigation was implemented to allow users to quickly find the information that was needed. A black and white color palette was deployed in order to keep the users attention on the imagery and the wide range of colors implemented in each design.
