KENOR

Brochure Design

This brochure was designed for Kenor, a Spanish artist, and his exhibition opening at MOCA. While creating each work of art, the artist listens to a record, then constructs a visual interpretation of what he heard.

Following Kenor’s abstract approach, the brochure incorporates his circular designs, while using a double gate fold to display the work in the form of its source. A take away insert was also added as a way to deliver tickets to the exclusive event opening.
