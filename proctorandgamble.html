PROCTOR AND GAMBLE

LOGO RE-DESIGN

Founded in 1837 by James Gamble, a soap maker, and William Procter, a candle maker, Procter and Gamble has become one of the world’s largest and most succesfull companies.

Using Chalet London 1970 as the inspiration, the P and G come together to form the ampersand that unites the founder’s names and serves as a reminder of how the two companies became one.
