JOURNALS

OPENINNG SEQUENCE

Journals, the documentary, is a compilation of interviews from people who knew the artist best and Cobain’s personal thoughts over the course of several years and in the month leading up to his tragic and controversial death. Kurt Cobain filled dozens of notebooks with lyrics, drawings, and writings about his plans for Nirvana and his thoughts about fame, the state of music, and the people who bought and sold him and his music. This documentary aims to reveal an artist who loved music, knew the history of rock, and who was determined to define his place history.

The opening sequence to this documentary, was meant to tell a story about one of the world’s most famous musicians and the his brief career. Although his life was cut short, the legacy and story that he left behind has captured the attention of millions of fans born before and after the rise of the band Nirvana. Iconic imagery from his career were broken down into multiple layers in order to show how the world would have looked without him, and the moments that would not have been complete without his presence.
