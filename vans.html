VANS

Corporate Identity

Since 1966, the Vans Shoe Company has been the premier manufacturer of shoes for extreme sports enthusiast.The brain child of Steve Van Doren, Vans has been creating iconic styles and prints that appeal to young and old alike. Their iconic sihlouttes adorn the feet of world famous atheletes and everyday people, with utility and performance as their goal.

With 2016 marking their 50th anniversary, a marketing campaign was developed to commemorate this important year in the company’s history while holding true to the brands iconic style. The campaign covered multiple aspects of material, logo, and print design.
